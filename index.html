The Yo-Yo Test

No one sleeps in just one position all night. You turn, shift, stretch, and roll over constantly—which means spending 10 seconds on a mattress in a showroom tells you very little.

That's where the Yo-Yo Test comes in.

Spend just 15 seconds in each of these four positions:

  • Left side
  • Right side
  • Back
  • Stomach

As you switch positions, pay attention to how your shoulders, hips, and lower back feel. A good mattress should provide consistent support and comfort no matter how you sleep—not just in your preferred position.

45 seconds. Four positions. One confident decision.
