Best 6 Mattresses in India for 2026

March 6, 2025Foamico Sleep Experts7 min read

Indian adults spend six to eight hours a night on a mattress, yet most of us give it less thought than we give a new phone. A poor mattress shows up as chronic back pain, disturbed sleep, and stiff mornings — and in India's climate, heat and humidity make the wrong choice even more noticeable. Here's how to think about it, and six ranges worth shortlisting this year.

What actually matters when choosing a mattress

  • Sleep position — side sleepers generally want more cushioning; back and stomach sleepers need firmer, flatter support
  • Body weight — heavier sleepers need a denser support core to avoid premature sagging
  • Climate — breathable materials matter more in India than in cooler markets
  • Budget — think in cost-per-night over the mattress's life, not just the sticker price
  • Trial period and warranty — look for a genuine trial window and a warranty long enough to matter
  • Motion isolation — worth prioritising if you share the bed

Six ranges worth shortlisting

Resto — cushioned on top, genuinely stable underneath. A dependable, everyday pick if you want firm orthopedic support without any fuss.

Sova — steady support with just the right amount of surface cushioning. Comes in Classic, Premium, and Luxury tiers, so you can dial in exactly how much comfort layering you want above the same trusted core.

Luma — gentle cushioning over a hybrid pocket spring core, for sleepers who want a bouncier, more breathable feel than an all-foam bed.

Ultima — engineered for consistent, long-term durability, backed by a 25-year warranty — one of the longest in our lineup.

Riva — plush on top while still keeping real structure underneath, available in three depths (1000, 2000, 3000) for however cloud-like you want the feel.

Pillows and accessories — a great mattress is only half the setup; pair it with the right pillow for your sleep position to get the full benefit.
